Mariah Carey is confirmed as the first artist to top the billboard hot 100 chart across four decades. She is referred to as the "Songbird Supreme" and the "Queen of Christmas"

Neanderthal flute is considered to be the oldest musical instrument in the world. It is made from the left thighbone of a young cave bear and has four pierced holes.

Leo Fender, the founder of the iconic electric guitar and bass brand and Rock and Roll Hall of Fame inductee, never learned to play either instrument.

Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart is the best and most famous composer of classical music. Born in Salzburg, in the Holy Roman Empire, Mozart showed prodigious ability from his earliest childhood.

The four main voices are typically labelled as soprano (or treble and countertenor), alto (contralto, countertenor or mezzo), tenor, and bass.
